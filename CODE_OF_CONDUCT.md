# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project is a learning-focused, respectful space for students and contributors. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and communicate clearly.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus discussions on learning outcomes and technical quality.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, intimidation, or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruptive or off-topic behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private issue in the repository and include relevant context.

All reports will be reviewed and handled fairly.
